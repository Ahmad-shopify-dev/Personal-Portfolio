Alpha Brand - Full Replatforming Journey

Alpha Brand approached us with a legacy WooCommerce store that was slow, difficult to update, and didn’t perform well on mobile devices. We proposed a full migration to Shopify 2.0 with a custom theme built from scratch using modular sections.

What We Did:

  • ✓ Replatformed from WooCommerce to Shopify
  • ✓ Built a fully modular custom theme
  • ✓ Designed and developed all sections from scratch
  • ✓ Added metafield support for dynamic content management
  • ✓ Optimized images and scripts for faster performance

Impact:

After launch, Alpha Brand saw a 25% improvement in speed and an 18% increase in mobile conversions within the first month. Their team now edits content without developer support.

Beta Co. - Building a Performance-First Shopify Store

Beta Co. needed a fast, SEO-friendly Shopify store that could be easily updated by non-developers. We designed and developed a theme from the ground up using Shopify 2.0 standards and metafields to give the admin full control.

Key Solutions:

  • ✓ Built homepage with reusable, dynamic blocks
  • ✓ Connected all content to metafields
  • ✓ Optimized theme structure and lazy loaded assets
  • ✓ Designed for both SEO and accessibility

Results:

Following the relaunch, Beta Co. experienced a 30% jump in organic traffic and a noticeable boost in usability. Their marketing team now launches pages without dev help.
